ABOUT US

OUR BACKGROUND, OUR VISION, & OUR MISSION.

Mabunda Group Pty. Ltd is the EHS consulting company which is geared to assist the clients to comply with OHS, Environmental, Food Safety and other applicable legislation and to empower all interested and affected stakeholders.

The company address the core EHS, Construction Health and Safety and Food Safety issues with all client’s employees at all levels and preferably at the language that they understand well.

Our aim is to promote a health and safe working environment in an inclusive- consultative approach at all spheres of management, all employees and interested or affected parties.

Knowledge and information is shared at the workplace to ensure continual improvement as required by International Standards. We aspire to be the preferred EHS, CHS and Food Safety Consultancy in industry.

  • Facilitate the Risk Assessment process.
  • Facilitate EHS Management Systems
  • Facilitate Environmental Health and Safety and Food Safety training.
  • Facilitate hands on practical solutions to the clients’ EHS, CHS and Food Safety challenges.
  • Providing professional advice to the convenience of the clients.
  • Providing appropriate advice in relation to EHS, Environmental, Food Safety and all applicable legislation and standards.
  • Conducts needs analysis in relation for EHS, CHS and Food Safety matters.
